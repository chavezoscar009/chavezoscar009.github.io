Data Science & ML Projects

A showcase of personal data science experiments and machine learning work outside my research that I dabbled with.

Golf Ball Hypothesis Testing

Performed statistical hypothesis testing to determine if a new golf ball design improves performance significantly. Explored p-values, confidence intervals, and data visualization to support conclusions.

Spectra Encoder & Decoder (CNN)

Developing a convolutional neural network to encode and decode spectral data efficiently. This project explores dimensionality reduction, reconstruction error, and neural network architectures for astronomical data.

Medical Imaging Classifier

Working on a CNN-based classifier for medical images to detect bone fractures and ear canal injuries. Goal is to automate injury detection and assess severity levels for faster diagnosis.
